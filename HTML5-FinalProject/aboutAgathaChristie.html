Agatha Christie: One of the Greatest Detective Novelists of All Time

Agatha Christie is an English author best known for her detective novels. With over 2 billion books sold, she remains the best-selling novelist of all time. Christie wrote 66 detective novels over her lifetime, and also a handful of stage plays and short stories.

Notable Works by Agatha Christie

  • And Then There Were None (1939)

  • Murder on the Orient Express (1934)

  • The ABC Murders (1936)
